Accounting Standards Applied

Lease Accounting
IFRS 16 Ind AS 116 ASC 842 HKFRS 16

Lessee recognises right-of-use asset and lease liability at PV of future payments. Interest via effective interest rate method. ROU asset depreciated straight-line.

Foreign Exchange
IAS 21 IFRS 21 Ind AS 11

Monetary items translated at closing rate. Exchange differences to P&L. Non-monetary items at historical rate. Parallel ledgers in USD, GBP, INR, SGD.

Industry Standards
IATA AAG IAS 37 IAS 16

IATA Airline Accounting Guidelines for chart of accounts and revenue recognition. IAS 37 for maintenance reserves. IAS 16 for owned aircraft.

Financial Instruments & Tax
IFRS 9 IAS 23 IAS 12 IAS 7

IFRS 9 for fuel hedges. IAS 23 for borrowing costs. IAS 12 for deferred tax on FX and lease differences. IAS 7 for cash flow presentation.
